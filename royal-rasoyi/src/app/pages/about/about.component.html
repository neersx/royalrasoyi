What Makes Us Special?

1. Nutritious Ingredients

  • Multigrain Atta: Our chapatis are crafted from a blend of raagi, makka, bajra, chana, oats, and wheat, providing a rich source of fiber and essential nutrients.
  • Premium Rice: We use high-quality rice for our pulav, ensuring every grain is packed with flavor and nutrition.
  • Fresh Vegetables: Only farm-fresh vegetables make it into our dishes, guaranteeing maximum freshness and health benefits.

2. Homemade Spices

All our spices, including garam masala, coriander powder, and black pepper, are ground in-house to deliver authentic flavors without any artificial additives.

3. Quality and Purity

  • Cow Ghee: Known for its rich taste and numerous health benefits, our dishes are prepared using pure cow ghee.
  • Olive Oil: A heart-friendly choice for lighter, healthier meals.
  • Pink Salt (Rock Salt): A healthier alternative to regular table salt, packed with essential minerals.
  • Mineral Water: All our cooking is done with pure mineral water, ensuring the highest standards of hygiene and purity.
